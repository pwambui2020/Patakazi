About Us

Patakazi service application is a first generation application that allows users to register with us..This applications allows you to get a chance to work with people who want your services in your area and beyond.This application allows the client to select and have someone do their job where they want.It is a means to empower the unemployed and those who have services skills like ;mechanics,photographer ,graphic designer,carpenter,tailor and etc,yet they don't have a stable job,or they want to work as a freelance.Moric came up with the idea as in his area most people are skilfull in certain area yet they lack exposure to the right people.This is what we want to curb,as Patakazi-App.
